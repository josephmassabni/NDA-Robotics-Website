2018-2019: Our Return to Glory

After the departure of 3 teams composed of seniors, and countless memories made, the NDA Robotics program has set it sights on a goal not reached last year- making it to the State Tournament. To do this, head of robotics program Mr. Yang emphasized a process called "reverse-engineering" to understand the positives and negatives of the robots built last year. This process has never been done before in our program's history, and will hopefully allow for a newfound sense of design that was not present last year. With the addition of 3 new freshmen teams, there will be new ideas, new experiences, and new competitions. This will also be the last year we will be using the legacy VEX cortex system, and will be upgrading to the V5 system next year, with the use of a grant!





The Game: Turning Point



Combining an exhilarating projectile aspect with flippable objects, Turning Point looks to be a competition that has never been seen before. Teams will have 3 choices: focus on the projectiles and flags, focus on the caps, or try their best to do both. The combination of an offensive and defensive game is sure to excite!

The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing Alliance by High Scoring or Low Scoring Caps, Toggling Flags, and by Alliance Parking or Center Parking Robots on the Platforms. Caps can be Low Scored on the field tiles, or High Scored on Posts, for the Alliance whose color is facing up at the end of the match. Flags can be Toggled to red or blue, and are Scored for the Alliance whose color is Toggled at the end of the match. Low Flags can be Toggled by Robots, but High Flags can only be Toggled by Balls. Turning Point is intended to be a back and forth game, no scored object is safe! Alliance Platforms can be used for Alliance Parking by Robots of the same color Alliance as the Platform. The Center Platform can be used by Robots from either Alliance for Center Parking. An additional bonus is awarded to the Alliance that has the most total points at the end of the Autonomous Period.
