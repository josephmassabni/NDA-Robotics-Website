At NDA, we aren't just involved with robotics. We are:



Role Models

Notre Dame Academy serves as the only Catholic high school in Green Bay, continuing an education rooted in faith for 9 Catholic primary schools in the area.

Through our example, the many middle school students in the area are inspired to persue a STEM education and prepare themselves for the future. Every year, students at the nearby middle schools share their scientific discoveries at our science fair.

Learn More
Leaders

Through our robotics program, students learn to become leaders, by incorporating all members' points of view and making decisions that benefit everyone.

All of our classes at Notre Dame Academy teach students to become aware of everyone, regardless of their importance on the team. At NDA, we learn from each other!

Volunteers

Service is one of the main aspects of a Notre Dame Academy. In the 2017-2018 school year, students recorded over 15,000 service hours, making an impact in society.

Many members of the robotics program are actively involved with service, from helping tutor math at the nearby middle schools to raising money to help educate students for years to come.

A fantastic compilation of sources we teach students with!



PROS

A 100% free, open-source development software for the VEX Cortex and V5 Multicontroller. Fantastic tutorials and general proramming knowledge that can help anyone learn to program for VEX! All members of our team proudly use PROS. Learn More

VEX Forums

The official forums related to everything VEX Robotics! Many users there are happy to help, as well as VEX officials that provide explanations of rules and other information. Making an account is easy and allows you to join the community! Learn More

Mr. Z's Video tutorials

Explanations to all of the VEX basics in video form! This 7-video series provides everything you need to start learning. Uses with RobotC, the most popular VEX development software. Learn More

CMU Robotics

The CMU Robotics Academy provides an in-depth look into both VEX IQ and VEX EDR. Provides information for teachers and students looking to form a robotics team! Some lessons are available for purchase, but many are also available for free. Learn More
