What makes Notre Dame Academy Robotics Different?

Faith

One of the most crucial aspects of our education at Notre Dame Academy, faith plays an important role in our robotics program. We seek to make the best use of our opportunities we have been given. We live by this motto: "Just try to make one person's day better!" We are the future generation, and it is up to us to make the world a better place.

Fun

It is clear that robotics competitions can become very stressful and intenese. However, our program emphasizes the learning opportunities regardless of winning or losing. We feel that robotics serves as a great experience for real life: something can always go wrong, but it is up to us fix it the best we can.

Tradition

There are many aspects of our robotics program that makes us unique. From our unique API found in the programming of our robots to the same materials being reused to keep our environmental footprint to a minimum, we keep a focus not only on the future, but also in what we have done in the past. No contribution, no matter how small or long ago, is not used: everyone is involved!



What is VEX Robotics?

VEX Robotics, the largest robotics competition in the world, is more than just building robots. VEX Robotics teaches real life STEM applications that prepare students for success in a world where technology becomes more and more critical. The VEX Robotics Competitions, also refered to as the "Greatest 2 Minutes in Robotics," are a series of competitions presented by the Robotics Education and Competition (REC) Foundation, a non-profit organization that is committed to forging young scholars into leaders in the STEM field. With competitions in over 40 countries and over 20,000 registered teams, it is clear that the REC Foundation has been able to inspire and educate learners throughout the world. Learn More



How can I get involved?

Great- welcome aboard! Here is how you can get involved with VEX:

Compete

If you want to dive right in, there are three different competitions just for you: VEX IQ, mainly intended for students in elementary and middle school, and VEX EDR, a competition for high schoolers and beyond! Many teams are looking for players- see if there is a robotics club in your school; if there isn't, you could definitely start one!

Volunteer

In the world of VEX Robotics, there is always an event somewhere. Chances are, there could be an event near you that is in need of volunteers! No experience? No problem- this is one of the best ways to learn more about robotics, and help others while doing it! By volunteering, you can help the schools that run events to raise money for their program, therefore helping a school directly as well.

Educate

If you think you know your stuff, you can definitely help a large amount of people! One easy way to do this would be to help out at the VEX Forums, the official forums of VEX Robotics. This would be a great way to help out beginners as well as more advanced teams.
