2017-2018: The End of a Dynasty

This year, we will be saying goodbye for 3 of our senior teams. It was a fantastic 4 years with them filled with successes, failures, and new discoveries. One of our teams, FORTE 6001D, was one of the most successful teams in program history, making it to states twice and even nationals. The other senior teams were also very successful, making it to states. All three teams were very dedicated, coming in to work on their robots during their study halls and after school. But, we will be gaining a new freshmen team that hopes to continue in their successful footsteps. This year we will be trying our best to end the dynasty on a high note, by making it to state and hopefully even further.





The Game: In the Zone



This year's game, "In the Zone", is something that has never been done before in VEX Robotics. Compared to last year, it is a shock to have all 4 robots on the same plane, where they have the possibility to prevent each other from moving and scoring. This implements a unique defensive strategy, as a team could follow their opponent and quickly unscore their hard work. Furthermore, this new game will have many different methods of scoring; from stacking cones to placing them on the high towers, the possibilites are endless!

Matches are played on a field set up as illustrated in the figures throughout. Two Alliances – one “red” and one “blue” – composed of two Teams each, compete in each Match. The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing Alliance by Stacking Cones on Goals, by Scoring Mobile Goals in Goal Zones, by having the Highest Stacks, and by Parking Robots.
